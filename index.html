About the Workshop

The Workshop on AI and the future of Collaborative Innovation (WAICI) brings together scholars from information systems, marketing, strategy and other disciplines to examine how artificial intelligence is transforming collaborative innovation across industries and institutions. Our focus encompasses the evolving landscape where AI technologies intersect with human collaboration, organizational innovation processes, and the broader ecosystem of knowledge creation and sharing.

Research Topics

We invite submissions on topics related to how modern AI technologies are affecting collaboration and innovation, including but not limited to:

Human–AI Creative Collaboration
How people and generative models co-produce ideas, content, and innovation
AI and Workplace Innovation
AI agents as teammates; intra-team dynamics; heterogeneous adoption
Productivity & Performance with AI
Real vs. perceived productivity gains; measurement of AI's effect on knowledge work
AI Agents in Markets and Marketplaces
Agentic buyers/sellers, market design, and platform economics
Foundation Models & Input Innovation
Prompting, fine-tuning, open-source LLMs, and data/API access
Marketing and Engagement Innovation
GenAI in advertising, content, recommendations, and consumer interaction
AI and Workforce Transformation
Skills, careers, labor markets, and the future of work in an agentic era
AI in/and Policy Innovation
Algorithmic fairness, governance, and regulatory impacts of AI
Methodological Innovations with LLMs
Synthetic respondents, data annotation, simulations, and AI-augmented research
